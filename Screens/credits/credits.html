About the Game

The Bunny is a modified point-and-click adventure that encourages users to type inputs in order to access information rather than clicking on hotspots.

We have ultimately opted out of using a game engine in favor of coding exclusively in HTML, CSS, and JavaScipt. This was a decision that went back and forth in our design procecss as we changed our ideas about what our project was going to ultimately be.
